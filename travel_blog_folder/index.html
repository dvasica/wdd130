Welcome to my Blog!

Hi Guys! My name is Dusan, I am coming from Serbia, and I am obsessed with traveling. Ever since I was a kid I would love to hear stories from my parents about their adventures and that is when I fell in love with traveling. Over the past 10 years, I visited quite a few countries. Here is the list of them: Spain, France, Germany, Italy, Hungary, Netherlands, Belgium, Romania, Croatia, Montenegro, Slovakia, Taiwan, Mexico, USA. That is 3 continents so far, next on my bucket list is South America. I wanted to start this blog so that I can share some of my favorite memories about trips I had over the years. I will start with Spain and Taiwan as some of my favorit e vacations and eventually I will include other countries as well.
